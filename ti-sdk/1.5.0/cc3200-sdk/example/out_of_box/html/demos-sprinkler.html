It's raining outside and you forgot to turn off the sprinkler. No problem. This demo shows you how SimpleLink CC3200 can be used to control the sprinkler system via the cloud.

Simply click on the On/Off buttons you see on your screen, you will see the sprinkler turn on and off. You can also see the LED lights flashing on and off.

Did you know?

CC3200 has low-power consumption due to its innovative one-chip architecture. The Wi-Fi sensors can run on 2xAA batteries for over 1 year!
